⚙️ Setup Guide

Connect Your Contact Form
to Gmail

Follow these 4 steps and every client who fills your contact form will send an email notification directly to your Gmail inbox — takes under 5 minutes.

1
Create a Free Formspree Account
⏱ 1 min

Formspree is the service that connects your HTML form to your Gmail. It's free for up to 50 submissions/month (plenty to start with).

👉 Go to: formspree.io → Click "Get Started" → Sign up with your Gmail address.

💡
Sign up using your Gmail address (shakeebiqbals@gmail.com) so notifications are sent directly to that inbox.
2
Create a New Form & Copy Your ID
⏱ 1 min

Once logged in to Formspree:

1. Click "+ New Form"
2. Name it: AI Solutions Contact
3. Set email to: shakeebiqbals@gmail.com
4. Click "Create Form"

You'll see a form ID in the URL or on the dashboard — it looks like this:

Your Formspree Form URL (example)
https://formspree.io/f/xpwzgkrb ↑ this is your Form ID — copy it!
📋
The Form ID is always 8 characters (letters + numbers). Copy just that part — e.g. xpwzgkrb
3
Paste Your Form ID into index.html
⏱ 1 min

Open your index.html file in any text editor (Notepad, VS Code, etc.) and find this line:

Find this (line ~1069 in index.html)
action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID"

Replace YOUR_FORM_ID with your actual ID:

Replace with (example)
action="https://formspree.io/f/xpwzgkrb"

Save the file. That's it — the form is now connected!

✅
Keyboard shortcut: In any text editor, press Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F on Mac) and search for YOUR_FORM_ID to find it instantly.
4
Test It & Verify Email Arrives
⏱ 2 min

Upload your updated index.html to your website host, then:

1. Open your website and scroll to the contact form
2. Fill it in with a test name + your own email
3. Click "Book Service Now"
4. Check your Gmail inbox — you should receive an email like this:

First submission? Formspree may send you a confirmation email first asking you to verify the form. Click the confirmation link, then test again.
🎉
It's working! From now on, every client who fills your contact form will trigger an instant Gmail notification with all their details.
