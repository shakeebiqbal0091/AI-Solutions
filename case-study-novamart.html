🔴 The Problem

E-commerce stores across Saudi Arabia lose customers daily due to slow support response times. Customers ask the same questions repeatedly shipping times, return policies, payment methods, order status tying up support staff and increasing operational costs.

NovaMart's support team was spending 6+ hours daily answering identical questions, leaving complex issues unresolved and customers frustrated. Peak hours evenings and weekends had no coverage at all.

🟢 The Solution

We built Nova an AI customer support agent trained on NovaMart's complete knowledge base. Nova handles all incoming customer queries instantly, 24/7, in both English and Arabic with zero human involvement for standard requests.

  • ✦ Answers shipping, returns, payments, and order questions instantly
  • ✦ Automatically detects customer language and responds accordingly
  • ✦ Escalates complex issues to human agents with full context
  • ✦ Deployed on web embeddable on any store platform
  • ✦ Trained on store-specific data not generic responses
