FAQ

Common Questions

Is the discovery call really free?

Yes, completely. The 30-minute discovery call has no obligation — we use it to understand your situation and determine whether a full strategy engagement would be valuable for you. Many clients get actionable insights from the free call alone.

Do we have to use you for implementation?

Absolutely not. The strategy report is yours to use however you like — with us, with another vendor, or with your internal team. We'd love to implement it, but there's no obligation.

How long does the full strategy engagement take?

From kickoff to final report delivery, typically 1–2 weeks. The operations workshop takes 90 minutes of your team's time. Everything else is done by us.

What size businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses from 5-person startups to 500-person enterprises. The strategy format scales to your complexity — a 5-person team gets a focused 3-opportunity roadmap, a 200-person company gets a full department-level analysis.
