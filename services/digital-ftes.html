What Is a Digital FTE?

A Full Job Role, Automated
End to End

A Digital FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) isn't just a tool — it's a complete job function, run by AI. We design it with a persona, train it on your processes and systems, integrate it into your stack, and it takes ownership of its role the same way a human employee would.

The difference: it works 24/7, never makes the same mistake twice, costs a fraction of the salary, and can handle 10× the volume of a human counterpart. Your newest hire is also your most reliable one.

FAQ

Common Questions

Will customers know they're talking to AI?

That's your choice. We can configure the FTE to be transparent about being AI, or to operate as a branded agent with no explicit AI disclosure. Many clients with luxury products choose the latter — the quality is indistinguishable.

What happens when it encounters something it can't handle?

The FTE has a defined escalation protocol — it pauses, notifies the right human on your team with full context, and waits for instruction before proceeding.

Can it work alongside my existing human team?

Absolutely. Digital FTEs are designed to complement your team — handling high-volume, repetitive work so your humans can focus on the complex, high-value work only they can do.

How do I update it when our processes change?

Updates are included in the monthly support. Tell us what changed, and we'll update the FTE's training and logic within 48 hours.
