What Is It?

More Than Automation True Intelligence

Traditional automation follows rigid rules: if X happens, do Y. Agentic AI is different. These agents can reason about goals, break them into sub-tasks, use tools (email, search, CRM, databases), and adapt when something unexpected happens.

Think of it as a tireless employee who can read, write, research, make decisions, and take action across any system without ever needing exact instructions. You give the goal. The agent figures out how.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use an AI agent?

Not at all. We handle all the technical architecture, setup, and maintenance. You simply tell us your business goals and we build a system that achieves them.

How long does deployment take?

For most single-workflow agents, we deliver a working system within 2 weeks of kickoff. Complex multi-agent systems typically take 3–5 weeks.

What happens if the agent makes a mistake?

Every agent has escalation logic built in if it encounters a situation it's not confident about, it pauses and notifies your team with full context rather than acting incorrectly.

Can it integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. We've built integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Notion, Slack, Airtable, Shopify, Google Workspace, Stripe, and dozens more. If it has an API, we can connect to it.
